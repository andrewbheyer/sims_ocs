======
Seeing
======

This table contains the seeing information from observations taken from Cerro Pachon for one year.

.. list-table:: 
    :header-rows: 1

    * -  Column
      -  Description
    * -  seeingId
      -  The numeric identifier for the particular seeing entry.
    * -  Session_sessionId
      -  The simulation run session Id.
    * -  s_date
      -  The time (units=seconds) from the zero point of the seeing data.
    * -  seeing
      -  The FWHM of the atmospheric PSF (units=arcseconds)
